# Sample Superstore Profit Report

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a deep dive into the profitability of a retail superstore. It analyzes sales performance across different regions, time periods, and product categories to identify growth opportunities and loss-making segments.

## Key Insights from the Dashboard
* **Sales Performance:** Total sales reached **185K** with a total profit of **23.0K**.
* **Regional Analysis:** The **West region** is the most profitable, contributing **36.31%** (8.35K) of total profits.
* **Product Categories:** The **Technology** category leads in profitability (**47.52%**), while **Chairs** are the top-performing sub-category.
* **Profit Trends:** The "Sum of Profit by Year and Quarter" line chart reveals significant seasonal fluctuations, with a major peak in 2018.

## Technical Skills Applied
* **Data Visualization:** Built interactive doughnut charts, line graphs, and matrix tables.
* **DAX & Measures:** Created calculations for Sum of Sales, Profit, and Quantity.
* **Data Formatting:** Applied a high-contrast dark theme for better readability and professional presentation.
